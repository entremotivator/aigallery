Welcome to Art Of Niyakire Gallery

Art of Niyakire Gallery is owned by Nelson Niyakire a visual artist, exhibition curator, and co-creator of Maison Beaulier. With a visionary approach, he crafts immersive exhibitions that blend creativity and curation, inviting viewers on unforgettable artistic journeys. His dedication to fostering innovation and beauty shapes environments where art flourishes and connections thrive.
